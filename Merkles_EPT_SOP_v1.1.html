Overview
Purpose & Scope
This SOP defines how the Merkles team operates its recruitment back-office function for Esha Parama Technology (EPT) in Indonesia. It applies to all Merkles team members at every stage of the recruitment cycle.
24h

85%
EPT's position fill rate — the bar Merkles is measured against
About EPT
Esha Parama Technology (EPT) is a Jakarta-based recruitment and staffing firm established in 2017. They serve 100+ clients across banking, insurance, financial services, IT, automotive, telecommunications, retail, and more. EPT's Indonesia office is at Office 88 Kasablanka Tower A, South Jakarta. Their India office is in HSR Layout, Bangalore.
EPT's service model includes contract staffing, permanent staffing, contract-to-hire, RPO, executive search, payroll solutions (99.6% accuracy), and IT solutions. Merkles currently supports their contract staffing and sourcing operations.
Merkles' role: We are EPT's India-based recruitment back-office. We operate under EPT's brand and process standards. Everything we produce — CVs, emails, candidate submissions — reflects on EPT's reputation with their clients.

Team Structure & Responsibilities
The Merkles team has three internal roles plus the EPT BD Team on the client side. Each role owns specific steps in the process.

Merkles SourcingSourcing TeamLogs roles, sources from Jobstreet and LinkedIn, does initial CV match, tags in CRM
Merkles RecruiterRecruiterPhone prescreen, salary collection, CV package preparation, confirmation email, interview coordination

Step 1 · EPT BD Team
Receive the Job Description
● EPT BD Team sends — Merkles Sourcing receives
EPT BD Team sends the requirement by email. Do not begin any sourcing activity until all required information is confirmed.
What must be in the JD before you proceed
  • Full Job Description with mandatory skills clearly listed
  • Budget stated as "Budget to Consultant" — all-inclusive, whether IC or PKWT
  • Client name and industry
  • Employment type — IC or PKWT
  • Work location and work type (WFO / WFH / hybrid)
  • Expected start date or urgency level
If anything is missing: Reply to EPT BD and ask before doing anything else. Do not guess or assume. A sourced candidate who does not fit the budget wastes everyone's time.
Step 2 · Merkles Sourcing
Log the Role in CRM
● Merkles Sourcing Team
Create a new role record in the CRM the same day the JD arrives.
What to record
  • Job title — use exact wording from the JD
  • Client name and industry
  • Employment type (IC or PKWT)
  • Budget ceiling — gross, all-inclusive
  • Work location and type
  • Date received from EPT
  • Status: set to Active — Sourcing
File the JD
Save the original JD in the shared folder. Naming convention:
EPT_[RoleName]_[ClientName]_[Date]
Example: EPT_FrontendDeveloper_AlloBank_050526
Why this matters: EPT tracks turnaround time. Delays in logging mean delays in reporting and sourcing — and delays mean missed the 24-hour target.
Step 3 · Merkles Sourcing
Source Candidates
● Merkles Sourcing Team
Sourcing channels and rules
  • Internal CRM first — always check before going to external job boards. You may already have a tagged candidate from a previous search
  • Jobstreet — primary Indonesia job board. Hard limit: 100 CV views per recruiter per year. Only open profiles you intend to act on
  • LinkedIn Recruiter Lite — shared team account. Coordinate internally to avoid messaging the same candidate twice
Jobstreet limit: 100 views per recruiter per year — roughly 2 per week. Do not browse casually. Every view counts against your annual limit.
Indonesia sourcing tips
  • Indonesian IT candidates often list skills only in a summary section — not in job descriptions. Flag these as "skills not contextualised" in the CRM note
  • Notice periods: typically 1 month for contract / IC, up to 3 months for permanent. Note this on the candidate record
  • Always record the candidate's current location and WFO willingness
  • Note tenure at each company — frequent short stints need explaining on the call
Step 4 · Merkles Sourcing
Match CV to JD
● Merkles Sourcing Team
Before tagging any candidate, verify all five criteria below. If any one fails, do not tag — go back to sourcing.
Matching checklist
  • Mandatory skills from the JD appear in the candidate's actual job experience sections — not only in the skills list at the top of the CV
  • Years of relevant experience meet the JD requirement
  • Current or last known salary appears to be within 30% below the budget ceiling
  • Candidate is in the right city or has indicated willingness to relocate / work WFO as required
  • Candidate profile shows openness to IC or PKWT as required by the JD
Do not tag a partial match. A weak submission wastes the Recruiter's prescreen time and damages Merkles' credibility with EPT. It is better to source longer and submit better.
Skills only in skills list? Flag the candidate as "skills not contextualised" in your CRM note. The Recruiter will verify on the call — the candidate may still be worth prescreening.
Step 5 · Sourcing → Recruiter Handoff
Tag in CRM & Hand Off
● Merkles Sourcing tags · Merkles Recruiter picks up
Sourcing team — how to hand off correctly
  • Tag the candidate record in the CRM against the open role
  • Set status to: Ready for Prescreen
  • Add a sourcing note — where the candidate was found and any flags observed
  • Flags to note: skills not contextualised, high salary expectation, currently in PKWT contract, WFO reluctance, multiple active interviews
  • The Recruiter picks this up from the CRM — no need to message separately unless urgent
Handoff point: Responsibility passes from Merkles Sourcing to Merkles Recruiter here. Sourcing's job on this candidate is done until the CV preparation stage.
Recruiter — how to pick up correctly
  • Read the sourcing note before calling the candidate
  • Review the CV against the JD yourself before the call
  • Confirm salary appears within the 30% hike rule
  • Update CRM status to: Prescreen in Progress
Step 6 · Merkles Recruiter
Phone Prescreen Call
● Merkles Recruiter
Cover the following in this order on every prescreen call.
Call script — cover in this order
  • Introduce yourself and Merkles — be clear you are a recruitment firm working on behalf of a client
  • Confirm the candidate is genuinely open to a new opportunity right now
  • Describe the role — title, industry, employment type, location — without naming the client yet
  • Confirm mandatory skills and where they used them (which company, which project, which phase)
  • Ask for current gross salary and expected gross salary
  • Confirm official notice period
  • Ask if they are interviewing elsewhere and whether they have any current offers
  • Ask if they are willing to do a technical test if required
  • Confirm two interview availability slots
  • Rate English communication yourself 1 to 5 based on the call quality
Indonesia-specific notes
  • Candidates almost always quote net salary — you must convert to gross before recording anything
  • "I will think about it" often means no in Indonesian culture — probe gently with a direct follow-up question
  • Candidates rarely give a hard no on the phone — listen for hesitation, not just words
  • Never reveal the client name during the prescreen call
WhatsApp first: If the candidate does not answer your call, send a WhatsApp message immediately after. Indonesian candidates respond to WhatsApp faster than to calls from unknown numbers.
Step 7 · Merkles Recruiter
Collect Salary and Convert to Gross
● Merkles Recruiter
Critical rule: Indonesian candidates almost always give you their net (take-home) salary. You must convert this to gross before recording or submitting anything to EPT.
The 30% rule

Salary hike calculation — gross to gross only

Current gross (example)Rp 10,000,000
Maximum hike allowed30%
Maximum expected gross you can submitRp 13,000,000
How to convert net to gross
  • Open the EPT Salary and Tax Calculator spreadsheet
  • Use TER A for single / no dependants (PTKP TK/0)
  • Use TER B for married, no dependants (PTKP K/0)
  • Use TER C for married with dependants (PTKP K/1, K/2 or K/3)
  • Input the net figure the candidate gave you and read the gross equivalent
  • Record both current gross and expected gross in the CRM
If the 30% rule is exceeded or budget ceiling is hit: Flag to Kishore immediately. Do not proceed to CV preparation without his decision. Kishore will escalate to EPT management if required.
Step 8 · Recruiter + Sourcing Support
Prepare the CV Package
● Merkles Recruiter leads · Sourcing supports
Most common rejection reason: Skills and technologies listed only in the skills section at the top — not in the job experience sections. If this is the case, ask the candidate to revise and resubmit before you prepare the package.
Mandatory checklist — every item before sending to Kishore
  • Skills and technologies appear in the actual job experience sections of the CV — not only in the skills list at the top
  • Apply yellow highlight to all mandatory JD technologies wherever they appear in experience sections
  • Remove phone number, email address, and LinkedIn URL from the CV
  • Add salary details on every page — current gross and expected gross
  • Rename the file using the correct convention
File naming: EPT_[RoleName]_[CandidateFirstName]
Example: EPT_FrontendDeveloper_Ivano
Sourcing Team support at this stage
Sourcing can assist with formatting — applying highlights, removing contacts, renaming the file. The Recruiter is accountable for the final quality check before it goes to Kishore.
Do not send to Kishore until every item is checked. A rejected CV comes back to you and delays the EPT submission — which delays the 24-hour target.
Step 9 · Kishore
Kishore Reviews the CV Package
● Kishore
This is the quality gate before anything goes to EPT. Kishore reviews every CV submission before it leaves Merkles.
What Kishore checks
  • CV naming convention is correct
  • Yellow highlights are applied accurately to mandatory skills in experience sections — not the skills list
  • Contact details have been stripped
  • Salary figures appear on every page in gross terms
  • 30% hike rule is satisfied — or a documented reason exists
  • Budget ceiling is not exceeded — or escalation to EPT management has been arranged
  • Overall candidate quality genuinely matches JD requirements
If rejected
Kishore returns to the Recruiter with specific feedback. The Recruiter either fixes the CV or returns to sourcing a new candidate. The process repeats from the relevant step.
Why this gate exists: A poor submission reflects on the entire Merkles team — not just the person who prepared it. Kishore's review protects our reputation with EPT.
Step 10 · Kishore
Submit to EPT BD Team
● Kishore
What to include in the submission email
  • Formatted CV — named correctly, highlights applied, contacts removed
  • Completed EPT Candidate Details table
  • Current gross and expected gross salary clearly stated
  • Two interview availability slots
  • English communication rating (1 to 5)
  • Any relevant notes: competing offers, test readiness, relocation status, current contract end date
Email format
To: EPT BD Team contact
CC: Kishore
Subject: [Role] — Candidate Submission — [CandidateName]
Step 11 · EPT BD Team
EPT Presents to Their Client
● EPT BD Team — Merkles waits and stays warm
EPT BD reviews the submission and presents the candidate profile to their end client. This step is outside Merkles' control.
What Merkles does while waiting
  • Allow 2 business days before sending a polite follow-up email to EPT BD asking for status
  • Do not follow up daily — it signals impatience and damages the relationship
  • Keep the candidate warm: send a brief message — "We have submitted your profile and will update you shortly"
If the client is not interested
  • EPT will inform Kishore
  • Update candidate CRM status to: Not Proceeded
  • Ask EPT BD if they can share the reason — this improves future submissions
  • Return to sourcing a fresh candidate — repeat from Step 3
Indonesian candidate management: A candidate left in silence for more than 3 business days will start interviewing elsewhere. Keep them engaged even while you wait for EPT feedback.
Step 12 · Merkles Recruiter
Send the Confirmation Email
● Merkles Recruiter
Only send after EPT confirms the client wants to interview. Never send this email speculatively.
What the email must confirm
  • Client name and industry
  • Job title
  • Employment type (IC or PKWT)
  • Contract duration if applicable
  • Work location and type (WFO / WFH / hybrid)
  • Salary offered — gross
  • Benefits — or state Nil
  • Notice period expected
  • Interview date and time — or two available slots
  • Whether there is a technical test involved
Required reply from candidate
The candidate must reply with exactly:

"Confirmed"

or

"I confirm my interest and commitment"

Any vague reply like "okay sounds good" does not count. Follow up until you receive the exact wording.
Once the candidate confirms, terms are locked. No renegotiation is possible after this point — on salary, location, or any other condition.
After confirmation is received
  • Forward the actual email reply — not a summary — to EPT BD team
  • Subject: [Role] — Candidate Confirmation — [CandidateName]
  • Save a copy in the CRM against the candidate record
Why save it: If there is ever a dispute about what the candidate agreed to, this email is your evidence.
Step 13 · Merkles Recruiter
Brief the Candidate & Confirm Interview
● Merkles Recruiter
When EPT sends the confirmed interview slot, call the candidate immediately. Do not just send an email.
Pre-interview briefing — cover all of these on the call
  • Confirm the exact date, time, and platform
  • Join 10 minutes early on a laptop — not a phone
  • Stable internet connection, quiet room, good lighting, camera on
  • Dress professionally even for video interviews
  • Remind them about any technical test if one was mentioned
  • Re-confirm the client name and what the company does — brief them on the industry context
  • Tell them not to contact the client or EPT directly about feedback — all communication goes through Merkles
  • End the call with: "Has anything changed since we last spoke that I should know about?"
On the day of the interview
WhatsApp reminder: Send 30 to 60 minutes before the interview — "Just a reminder — your interview is today at [time]. Best of luck! I will check in with you after." This dramatically reduces no-shows in Indonesia.
  • Update CRM status to: Interview Scheduled
  • Log the interview date and time in the candidate record
  • Message the candidate after the interview to ask how it went
Step 14 · Merkles Recruiter
Post-Interview Follow-up
● Merkles Recruiter
Immediately after the interview date
  • Update candidate status to: Interviewed — Awaiting Feedback
  • Send a brief check-in to the candidate — ask how it went, note their impression
  • Wait 2 business days then follow up with EPT BD for client feedback
  • Follow up every 2 business days after that until you have a definitive answer
Possible outcomes
OutcomeWhat to do
Offer extendedCoordinate with EPT BD on offer details. Update CRM to: Offer Stage
Second interviewRepeat Steps 12 and 13
Candidate rejectedUpdate CRM to: Not Selected. Keep candidate warm for future roles
No EPT responseFollow up every 2 business days politely until definitive answer
Candidate withdrawsNote reason in CRM. Inform EPT BD. Return to sourcing
Always close the loop with the candidate regardless of outcome. A rejected candidate treated with respect becomes a referral source. This is especially important in Indonesian professional culture where reputation spreads quickly through networks.

These rules apply to every candidate submission without exception. Kishore enforces all of them at the review gate.

Indonesia Employment Types
Your team will encounter three employment types. The type affects salary calculation, candidate expectations, confirmation email content, and tax treatment.

IC — Independent Consultant

  • Not an employee — civil and commercial contract only
  • Payment is called a Fee, not a salary
  • Responsible for own tax (PPh 21 or PPh 23)
  • Not entitled to BPJS or leave benefits
  • No probation period, no severance
  • Most contract IT roles in Indonesia are IC

PKWT — Fixed Term

  • Formal employment contract — maximum 5 years total
  • No probation period allowed
  • Must be in writing in Indonesian language
  • Ends automatically when period expires
  • Generally no severance unless specified
  • If violated, may convert automatically to PKWTT

PKWTT — Permanent

  • No end date — permanent employment
  • Maximum 3 months probation
  • Entitled to severance pay on termination
  • Mandatory BPJS Kesehatan and Ketenagakerjaan
  • Minimum regional wage applies
  • Rare in Merkles' current scope
During prescreen: Always confirm which employment type the candidate is comfortable with. IC candidates may not want to be PKWT and vice versa — confirming this early avoids late-stage dropouts.

Do's & Don'ts
Quick reference for every Merkles team member.
Always do this
  • Log the role in CRM on the day it arrives
  • Check the internal CRM before going to Jobstreet
  • Convert salary to gross before recording anything
  • Flag any 30% hike exception to Kishore before proceeding
  • Ask the candidate to revise their CV if skills are only in the skills list
  • Keep the candidate warm with brief updates while waiting
  • Get the written email confirmation before the interview proceeds
  • Send a WhatsApp reminder 30 to 60 minutes before every interview
  • Always close the loop with the candidate regardless of outcome
  • Follow up with EPT BD every 2 business days if no feedback received
  • Coordinate with your team before messaging a candidate on LinkedIn
Never do this
  • Start sourcing before confirming all JD details
  • Open Jobstreet profiles out of curiosity — every view uses your annual limit
  • Submit net salary figures to EPT
  • Proceed with an over-budget candidate without Kishore's approval
  • Highlight skills that only appear in the skills list section
  • Reveal the client name during the prescreen call
  • Accept "okay sounds good" as a valid written confirmation
  • Leave a candidate in silence for more than 3 business days
  • Follow up with EPT BD daily — it damages the relationship
  • Let a candidate contact the client or EPT directly
  • Submit a CV to EPT without Kishore's approval
